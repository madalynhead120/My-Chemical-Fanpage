My Chemical Romance, an American rock band from New Jersey, exploded onto the scene in the early 2000s and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Formed in 2001, the band consists of Gerard Way, Mikey Way, Frank Iero, and Ray Toro. They have released four studio albums: I Brought You My Bullets, You Brought Me Your Love (2002), Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge (2004), The Black Parade (2006), and Danger Days: The True Lives of the Fabulous Killjoys (2010).

The band split up on March 22, 2013, after which the members went on to focus on their solo careers. In 2019, they started touring again and have been ever since. Currently, they are on probably the biggest tour of their careers, Long Live the Black Parade.

I started listening to MCR in 2022 and immediately became obsessed. They quickly consumed my whole life. My favorite MCR album is Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge. I saw them in Chicago on August 29, 2025 at Soldier Field Stadium and it was one of the best events I've experienced. Fun Facts:

  • The band's first song was Skylines and Turnstiles

  • Three Cheers for Sweet Revenge was a commercial success and launched them to fame

  • Thursday frontman Geoff Rickly produced their first album

  • Stephanie Meyer was inspired to write the Twilight series because of MCR

  • Liza Minnelli appears on the track "Mama"

  • The Black Parade was written in the Paramour Mansion, a supposedly haunted mansion

  • Gerard Way, Mikey Way, Ray Toro, and Matt Pelissier were the original founders of My Chemical Romance. Pelissier was kicked out for not practicing and Frank Iero didn't join until 2002, right before their debut album released. Bob Bryar (1979-2024) joined in 2004, not long after Revenge released and left in 2010 for undisclosed reasons

  • Gerard Way was inspired to start My Chemical Romance after witnessing the 9/11 attacks

  • Danger Days was a huge departure from the band's original sound and was divisive amongst fans upon release

  • My Chemical Romance released a new single in 2022, "The Foundations of Decay" which was their first new song released since 2014
